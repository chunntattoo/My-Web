Booking Process / 預約流程

Step-by-step Guide / 預約流程說明

01

Consultation & Concept / 諮詢構思

Please prepare your reference images, desired size (in cm), and placement details.
請準備好您的參考圖、希望的尺寸（公分）及位置細節。

02

Inquiry & Quote / 詢價報價

After filling out the booking form, we will provide a preliminary quote and estimated time.
填寫預約表單後，我們會根據圖案複雜度與尺寸提供初步報價。

03

Deposit / 支付訂金

Once confirmed, please pay the deposit to secure your appointment.
報價確認後，請支付訂金以正式保留您的預約時段。

Terms & Notice / 預約條款

Important Information / 預約條款須知

Deposit Policy / 訂金政策

Deposits are non-refundable. For rescheduling, please notify at least 48 hours before your appointment.
訂金支付後恕不退還。如需改期，請務必於 48 小時前通知。

Health & Age / 健康與年齡

No clients under 18. Please ensure you are in good health and have had enough sleep.
恕不接待未滿 18 歲者。施作當天請確保身體狀況良好。

FAQ / 常見問題

Common Questions / 常見問題解答

How to get a quote? / 如何獲得報價？ +
Pricing varies by detail and placement. Please provide full details in the form for an estimate.
價格根據精細程度、尺寸與位置而定。請透過預約表單提供詳細資料以供估價。
Do you do cover-ups? / 可以進行蓋圖 (Cover-up) 嗎？ +
Yes, but cover-ups depend on the existing tattoo's status. Please attach clear photos in the form.
可以，但蓋圖服務需視原圖狀況而定。請在表單中附上現有紋身的清晰照片以進行評估。
